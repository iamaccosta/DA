Second Scenario. A bin packing algorithm that organizes the Couriers and the Orders using a simple heuristic that measures how efficient it is to deliver certain Orders using certain Couriers, by measuring and utilizing the profit metric, taking into account the weight and volume and not only the cost/reward. Organizes in an ascending order the Couriers by the profit metric (cost divided by weight and volume, so lower is better) Organizes in a descending order the Orders by the profit metric (reward divided by weight and volume, so bigger is better) Upon that, starts to fill the Couriers checking their currentWeight and currentVol occupied. Prints the result of the algorithm.
